KNOWN SCIENTIFIC LIMITS — INTENTIONAL AND DISCLOSED IN THE SITE
- Statewide range data are not local occurrence records.
- Host relationships may have been documented outside Texas.
- Genus-level plant relationships and native-status guidance do not establish suitability of every species in the genus.
- The dashboard uses an August 26, 2025 UDELep snapshot; later source revisions may differ.
- Photos and live checklist badges require internet access and depend on iNaturalist availability.

REGIONAL iNATURALIST PHOTO REVISION
- Automatic photo search now uses three ordered stages:
  1. Central Texas priority bounding box: 29.30 to 31.05 latitude and -98.90 to -97.00 longitude
  2. The iNaturalist Texas place
  3. A clearly labeled outside-Texas worldwide fallback only when regional open-license photos are unavailable
- Applies to insect thumbnails, insect detail photos, plant cards, featured plants, My Garden, comparison cards, and plant spotlights
- Thumbnail badges identify CTX, TX, or Global source scope
- Full photo credits identify Central Texas observation, Texas observation, or Outside Texas fallback
- Only research-grade observations with CC0 or CC BY photo records are accepted by the automatic loader
- Photo and license links continue to point to the corresponding iNaturalist photo record
- The Central Texas box is disclosed as a practical search region, not an official ecological or political boundary
- No visitor geolocation is requested; only fixed regional filters and the selected taxon name are sent to iNaturalist
